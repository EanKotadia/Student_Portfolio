Newsify

In an era where misinformation spreads rapidly, Newsify stands as a trustworthy news platform that ensures you receive only verified and credible information. Our system aggregates news from multiple reliable publications and employs AI-powered verification to detect and filter out false or misleading content.

AI Verified News

Using machine learning and external fact-checking databases, Newsify examines articles for inconsistencies, biased reporting, and false claims. It also assigns a bias score and using refrences assigns the leaning of the news media.

Image Fact-Check

Misinformation often spreads through doctored images. Newsify analyzes metadata and reverse-searches images to confirm their credibility.

Multi-Source News Aggregation

We compile stories from a variety of verified publications, ensuring diverse perspectives while prioritizing accuracy over virality.
