Newsify - A platform that brings and verifies news from various articles, allowing users to upload their own news for verification by our algorithm.

Description

This platform brings and verifies news from various articles, it also allows users to upload their own news for verification by our algorithm.

Frontend

HTML, CSS, JS

Backend Database

Firebase

How It Works

Newsletter Platform: Brings news from NewsAPI and uses Google Fact Check Algorithm to verify that news, also uses image checking to see if the image is fake or dissociated with the main article.

Check/Verify Your Own News: Allows users to upload their own news anonymously for checking by our algorithm.
